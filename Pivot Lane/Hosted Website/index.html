Immigration Support

Employment Visa & Work Permit Processing

PivotLane provides efficient coordination and administrative support throughout the immigration process, ensuring applications are prepared accurately and submitted in a timely manner. We handle the complexity so you can focus on your business.

1

Initial Information Collection

We collect your business documents, set up your company agent profile with ISS, and assess all requirements for the application.

2

Document Collection

We provide a comprehensive checklist and assist in gathering all required documents including passports, employment contracts, police clearance, medical reports, and certificates.

3

Application Preparation

Documents are reviewed for completeness, accuracy, and compliance. We identify and correct any missing information before submission.

4

Submission

Applications are filed and coordinated with the relevant authorities, addressing any additional document requests or queries that arise during processing.

5

Monitoring & Follow-Up

We track your application progress, respond to authority queries on your behalf, and provide regular status updates throughout the process.

6

Approval & Completion

Upon approval, we guide you through the final steps including visa stamp uploads and work permit endorsement at the Passport Office.
